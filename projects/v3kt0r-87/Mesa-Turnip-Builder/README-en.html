Important : Android 15 (SDK 35) is needed for Full Vulkan 1.4 support

MAKE SURE YOU HAVE A STABLE INTERNET CONNECTION BEFORE PROCEEDING

USE A VPN IF YOUR ISP THROTTLES YOUR SPEED
