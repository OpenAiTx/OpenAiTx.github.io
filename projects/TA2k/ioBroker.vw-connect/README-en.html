ioBroker.vw-connect

Tests::

vw-connect adapter for ioBroker

Adapter for VW We Connect, We Connect ID, We Charge, myAudi, Skoda Connect, Seat Connect and We Connect Go

Please update your system to Node 10.

Usage

Use the state under remote control to control your car remotely. Normal refresh is the polling interval to receive data from the VAG Cloud Force refresh is for non E-Cars to enforce a refresh; this number is limited by VAG until the car is turned on again. Trip data is only available for non E-Cars.

You can set climatization temperature in .climater.settings.targetTemperature.content
